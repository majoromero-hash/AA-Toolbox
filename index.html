A&I Advanced Analytics: Methods

Source: A&I Advanced Analytics: Methods
Topic Analysis Type Business Questions

Customer engagement

 Affinity prediction How can we predict the best content and channel for customers, including those with limited data?
Journey design How can we sequence tactics to optimize campaign effectiveness and improve customer experience?
Next best action What is the recommended next best message and from what channel?
Customer segmentation How can we understand groups of customers with similar key drivers and motivations?

Patient treatment

 Patient journey How can we predict propensity to switch or discontinue early? Where are there gaps in diagnosis? What are key issues/themes during different stages of the patient journey?
Patient segmentation Which groups of patients have similar characteristics based on individual attributes and/or patient journeys?
Location & travel Where are different patient segments located? How can we understand travel burden? Where should campaigns be targeted and tailored to the local population?
Access Where are there disparities in treatment access and outcomes?

Resource optimization

 Field team sizing and deployment What resources are required and how can the territory alignments be optimized?
Customer & account prioritization Who are the most important customers to focus on based on potential? What is the optimal channel from an efficiency and customer engagement standpoint? What resources are required for different account segments?
Forecasting & variance detection Where do we need to focus attention based not only on historical trending but also on future outlook?
Impact analysis What is the success and quantified impact of various tactics? What are the KPIs and key drivers?

Healthcare ecosystem

 Location & catchment areas Where are treatment centres located and what are the catchment areas for clinical trials as well as commercial engagement?
System KPIs Where are there health care inefficiencies?
Network analytics How are stakeholders connected? Who are most influential? What are common interests and objectives? Where are there leverage points?
Stakeholder identification How can we mine and map key stakeholders (HCPs, government, etc.)?
Insights How can I quickly distill insights from large or fragmented text from many different sources? What are common terms mentioned? How can collections of text be clustered to understand common topics/themes?

Advanced reporting

 Trigger alerts How can I be alerted about a new customer? How can I find out if we fall below specified KPIs?
Information access / extraction How can I set up ad hoc analyses so they can be performed automatically on an ongoing basis?
Access How can I make data science outputs live and interactive?
Integration How can I view 360 profile info to support customer and territory planning?
Exploration What can ML/AI techniques tell me about my data without pre-defined questions?

Data curation

 Live access / integrated public data How can we leverage public websites and integrate data from those sources on an ongoing basis? What news feeds can we track to keep on top of evolving information?
Data wrangling How can we make better use of text and other unstructured data? (i.e. Excel files with multiple tabs, Word/PDF docs etc.)?
Local and global data access What secondary data is available to help with SIT, including global and public sources?
Data onboarding and storage How can disparate data sources be automatically integrated with other data?
